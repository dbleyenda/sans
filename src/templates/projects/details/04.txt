				<h2>Interactive museum concept</h2>
			</div>
			<div class="item">
				<span>The challenge</span>
			</div>
			<div class="item text">
				<p>The Ana Frank center in Argentina, provides an experience of awareness to teenagers and adults about what the Frank family lived during the Nazi Holocaust to the Jews. It also makes emphasis on discussing about how values are inculcated in modern society.</p>
				<p>The purpose of this work was to convert a room of the museum to an interactive experience. We chose to reframe the initial center room, which has a 5 wall timeline where young volunteer guides transmit their knowledge. We proposed an interactive tour, called “Memories &amp; thoughts”, where first the user gets in touch with what the Frank family had experienced, so then he/she can make your own opinion about it, record it on video and watch what others have shared. Also, during the interactive tour, the visitors can save content using a e-card and before leaving the museum they can send it to their e-mail for further reading at home.</p>
