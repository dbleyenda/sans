I'm a graphic designer graduated from the University of Buenos Aires with more than 10 years of experience designing and developing digital products. I have been involved in several design agencies and start-up companies helping them craft digital experiences.

I'm very attracted to industrial design projects, particularly with the ones that perform interaction design with objects. I'm also getting involved in the illustration field at the moment. Say hello via e-mail or LinkedIn.
