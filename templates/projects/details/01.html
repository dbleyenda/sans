Powering the Open Data economy

The challenge

In the information era, democracies are facing corruption, information leaks, worthless data, and monopolies. To face those threats, goverments need to transform their data into resources that engage citizens participation, can be compliant with multilaterals standards, gain political capital, and unlock economic value.

To satisfy those demands, we at Junar built an Open Data Platform which powerful capabilities make that non reusable, hard to find or unusable data get processed through a data engine that enables data publishing, presentation, search and usage. Our users can create an Open Data portal in an effortless iniciative, tell stories through data or let developers make apps using our built-in API.
