A chef's showcase

The challenge

Chilenian chef, José Martínez, works very hard to impress and satisfy their clients when he is contracted for an event. He produces almost any kind of food. The majority of his clients are recommended by a previous customer, so he came to the need to show his work to them, so he contacted me to build a showcase website.

Since he had no brand at all, I made a logo for him that he can use to put on work uniforms and food packagings he deliver to clients. For the website, I took photos to several of his food preparations, uploaded them to Flickr, and using it’s API, I built a showcase website for his work.

Also, I made business cards for him.
